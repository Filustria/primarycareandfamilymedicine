Categorizing Ambulatory-care sensitive conditions

This chapter is meant to explain the methods involved on categorizing Ambulatory-care sensitive conditions (ACSC) in this research. The framework used was the Brazilian list of Ambulatory-Care Sensitive Conditions (Br-ACSC) [2]. This list comprises 20 domains covering infectious conditions (pneumonia and bacterial infections), chronic health problems (angina, heart failure) and preventive health care procedures (prenatal care), providing a good framework to approach the FM broad scope of practice.

The 20 domains of the Br-ACSC and its respective ICD-10 codes are listed below:
